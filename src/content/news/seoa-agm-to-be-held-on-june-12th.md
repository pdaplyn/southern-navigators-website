The SEOA AGM will be open to all SEOA members, access via Zoom. details to follow nearer the time. 

Any proposed amendments to the constitution or nominations for election as officers of the SEAO should be sent to the secretary by Thursday 15 May (4 weeks before the meeting). Matters for inclusion on the AGM agenda can be submitted until 29 May (2 weeks before the AGM).
