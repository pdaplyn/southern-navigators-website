Congratulations to Frank, Mel, and Charlotte for winning the Club Championships!
            

	    		
    		    

They put together great races on varied and technical courses at Mole Valley's event on Headley Heath.

Mel won the women's event (Green) in a time of 40:27, ahead of Katherine in second and Lucy third.

Frank took his second SN champs title in a row, winning the Blue course in 45:13, ahead of Peter D. in second and Tim third.

And Charlotte was the fastest SN junior on the Orange course, winning the course outright by over three minutes. Ethan was second and Finn third.

Full results are now available on the Mole Valley website.
